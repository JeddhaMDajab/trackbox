How TrackBox Works

Our streamlined process ensures lost items find their way back to their rightful owners quickly and efficiently.

1. Report

Students and faculty can easily report missing belongings, or security staff can log found items into the centralized database.

2. Match

Our intelligent system processes the data and typically finds a potential match between a lost report and a found log within 60 seconds.

3. Claim

Once verified, claiming instructions are provided, and the owner can securely retrieve their item from the designated security office.
